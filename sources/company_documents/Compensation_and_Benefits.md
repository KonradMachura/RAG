# SuperTech Employee Handbook: Compensation and Benefits
*Last updated: January 2025*
*Applies to: All full-time employees of SuperTech Inc.*

## 1. Core Benefits Catalog

SuperTech provides a comprehensive benefits package accessed via the *BeneFlex* internal portal. Enrollment for new employees opens on the 1st day of the month following their start date.

### 1.1. Private Medical Care
* **Provider:** MediCare Plus.
* **Coverage:** Employees receive the "Premium" package fully funded by SuperTech. This includes unlimited access to general practitioners, specialists, and dental emergencies.
* **Family Members:** Employees can add a spouse, partner, or dependents to their plan for a subsidized flat rate of $45 per person, deducted directly from monthly payroll.

### 1.2. Sports and Wellness
* **Provider:** FitPass.
* **Usage:** Grants access to over 4,000 gyms, swimming pools, and yoga studios nationwide.
* **Cost:** SuperTech covers 80% of the monthly subscription fee. The employee contribution is $15/month.

### 1.3. Life Insurance
* **Provider:** SecureLife.
* **Details:** Basic life and accidental death insurance (coverage up to $100,000) is provided at no cost. Additional voluntary coverage can be purchased via *BeneFlex*.

## 2. Training and Development Budget

Continuous learning is a core value at SuperTech. 

### 2.1. Annual Allowance
Every full-time employee is entitled to a training budget of **$1,200 per calendar year**.

### 2.2. Eligible Expenses
* Industry conferences (tickets and travel).
* Professional certification exams (e.g., AWS, PMP).
* Online courses (Udemy, Coursera) and professional literature/books.

### 2.3. Reimbursement Process
1. Obtain pre-approval from your direct manager via email.
2. Purchase the item/ticket using your own funds or a virtual company card.
3. Submit the invoice through the *Spendesk* platform within 14 days of purchase.

### 2.4. Condition
If an employee voluntarily leaves the company within 6 months of a training expense exceeding $500, they are required to reimburse 50% of the cost.

## 3. Employee Referral Program

We believe our employees are the best source of new talent. 

### 3.1. Referral Bonuses
* Junior/Entry-level roles: **$500**
* Mid-level roles: **$1,500**
* Senior/Lead roles: **$3,000**
* C-Level or Highly Specialized roles: **$5,000**

### 3.2. Process
Resumes must be submitted via the *Greenhouse* ATS system using the employee's unique referral link. Sending a CV directly to HR does not qualify for the bonus.

### 3.3. Payout Conditions
The bonus is paid in two installments:
* 50% paid on the new hire's first day.
* 50% paid after the new hire successfully passes their 3-month probationary period.
