# SuperTech Employee Handbook: Employee Lifecycle
*Last updated: February 2025*

## 1. Onboarding Process

The first week at SuperTech is dedicated to integration and setup.

### 1.1. Day 1 (Welcome Day)
* Breakfast with the team.
* Hardware pickup from the IT Desk (2nd floor).
* System access configuration via the *Okta* Identity Portal.

### 1.2. Mandatory Training
Must be completed by the end of Week 1:
* Occupational Health and Safety (OHS/BHP) - Online module via *TalentLMS*.
* GDPR and Data Privacy Certification.

### 1.3. Buddy System
Every new hire is assigned a "SuperTech Buddy" for the first 30 days to help navigate company culture and informal processes.

---

## 2. Performance and Feedback

We focus on continuous growth through regular feedback loops.

### 2.1. Review Cycle
Formal performance reviews happen bi-annually (April and October) using the *Lattice* platform.

### 2.2. Promotion Criteria
Promotions and salary increases are based on:
1. Consistent achievement of role-specific KPIs over two consecutive quarters.
2. Demonstrated adherence to SuperTech's Core Values (Innovation, Empathy, Accountability).
3. Completion of a "Next-Level Readiness" assessment by the direct manager.

---

## 3. Offboarding Process

When an employee decides to move on, we aim for a smooth transition.

### 3.1. Resignation
Must be submitted in writing to the direct manager and HR.

### 3.2. Handover
A detailed handover document detailing daily tasks, open projects, and important contacts must be saved in the team's shared Confluence space at least 3 days before the final day.

### 3.3. Hardware Return
All company property (laptop, phone, access badges) must be returned to the IT Desk by 4:00 PM on the employee's last working day.

### 3.4. Exit Interview
A 30-minute confidential interview with HR to discuss the employee's experience and reasons for leaving.
