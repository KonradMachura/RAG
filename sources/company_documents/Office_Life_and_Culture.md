# SuperTech Employee Handbook: Office Life and Culture
*Last updated: November 2024*

## 1. Dog-Friendly Office Policy

SuperTech welcomes well-behaved dogs in the office to create a relaxed environment.

### 1.1. Prerequisites
Before bringing a dog to the office, the owner must submit a "Pet Passport" to HR, which includes:
* Proof of up-to-date vaccinations (specifically Rabies and DHLPP).
* A signed liability waiver.

### 1.2. Team Consent
You must obtain informal consent from all team members sitting in your immediate vicinity. If anyone has allergies or phobias, the dog cannot be brought to that specific desk area.

### 1.3. Zoning
Dogs are strictly prohibited in:
* The main cafeteria.
* Server rooms.
* Client-facing meeting rooms (Rooms A, B, and C on the 3rd floor).

### 1.4. Liability
The employee is fully responsible for any damage caused by their pet to company property or other employees' belongings.

## 2. Workspace Organization

SuperTech utilizes a hot-desking system to maximize space efficiency.

### 2.1. Desk Booking
Desks must be reserved using the *SpaceOS* mobile app. You can book a desk up to 14 days in advance. Dedicated desks are only provided to employees requiring specialized hardware (e.g., Video Editors).

### 2.2. Meeting Rooms
Must be booked via Google Calendar. If a room is not occupied within 15 minutes of the scheduled start time, the booking is automatically canceled by the room's tablet system.

### 2.3. Parking Spaces
We have 50 underground parking spots. 
* 10 are reserved for executives and visitors.
* 40 are available via a weekly lottery system in the *SpaceOS* app.

---

## 3. Dress Code

### 3.1. Everyday Wear
SuperTech enforces a **"Smart Casual"** dress code. Jeans, t-shirts, and sneakers are perfectly acceptable. We ask that clothing is clean and free of offensive graphics.

### 3.2. Client Meetings
When meeting with external clients or partners, employees are expected to upgrade their attire to "Business Casual" (e.g., button-down shirts, blazers, dress shoes).
