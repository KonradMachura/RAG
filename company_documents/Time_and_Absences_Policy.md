# SuperTech Employee Handbook: Time, Attendance, and Absences Policy
*Last updated: October 2024*
*Applies to: All full-time and part-time employees of SuperTech Inc.*

## 1. Working Models and Location Policy

SuperTech operates on a flexible working model designed to foster both collaboration and individual focus. Employees are assigned to one of three working models upon hiring.

### 1.1. Hybrid Work Model (Default)
The majority of SuperTech employees operate under the Hybrid Work Model.
* **Requirements:** Employees must be present in their designated home office for a minimum of 2 days per week (or 8 days per month). 
* **Core Hours:** Regardless of location, employees must be available during core business hours: **10:00 AM to 3:00 PM (CET)**.
* **Office Days Scheduling:** Office days must be logged in the *SpaceOS* application no later than Friday of the preceding week to ensure desk availability.

### 1.2. Fully Remote Work Model
Available only for specific roles approved by the Department Director.
* **Equipment:** Remote employees are eligible for a one-time Home Office Stipend of $500 for ergonomic equipment.
* **Quarterly Meetups:** Remote employees are required to attend mandatory quarterly department meetings in person. Travel and accommodation costs are covered by SuperTech.

### 1.3. Workation (Work from Anywhere Policy)
Employees may work from a location outside their country of employment for up to **45 days per calendar year**.
* **Conditions:** * The location must have a stable internet connection.
  * The time zone difference must not exceed +/- 3 hours from the employee's home office.
  * Approval from the direct manager must be obtained at least 14 days in advance via *Workday*.
  * Employees are responsible for their own tax and visa compliance regarding the destination country.

## 2. Leave Policy and Holidays

SuperTech encourages employees to take time off to rest and recharge. All leave requests must be submitted through the *Workday* HR system.

### 2.1. Annual Leave (Paid Time Off - PTO)
* **Entitlement:** All full-time employees are entitled to **26 days** of paid annual leave per calendar year. Part-time employees receive a prorated amount.
* **Approval Process:**
  * Leaves up to 3 days: Require 3 days' advance notice and direct manager approval.
  * Leaves longer than 3 days: Require at least 14 days' advance notice to ensure proper project handover.
* **Rollover Policy:** Up to 5 unused PTO days can be rolled over to the next calendar year but must be used by March 31st.

### 2.2. On-Demand Leave (Emergency Leave)
Employees are entitled to **4 days** of on-demand leave per year (deducted from the total 26 days of PTO).
* **Usage:** Used for sudden emergencies.
* **Procedure:** Must be reported to the direct manager and HR via email or Slack before the start of the employee's standard shift. No prior approval is required.

### 2.3. Unpaid Leave and Special Occasions
* **Unpaid Leave:** Can be requested for periods up to 3 months. Requires approval from both the Direct Manager and the HR Director.
* **Special Occasion Leave (Paid):**
  * Employee's wedding: 2 days.
  * Birth of a child (for non-birthing parent): 2 days (in addition to statutory paternity leave).
  * Bereavement (immediate family): 2 days.

---

## 3. Sick Leave and Medical Absences

Health comes first. If you are unwell, you are strictly prohibited from coming to the office to prevent the spread of illness.

### 3.1. Reporting Procedure
1. **Immediate Notification:** Notify your direct manager and your team via your team's Slack channel before 9:30 AM.
2. **Status Update:** Change your Slack status to the "Sick" emoji (🤒) and pause your notifications.
3. **Formal Documentation:** Upload your official medical certificate (e.g., e-ZLA in Poland) to *Workday* within 3 days of the start of the absence.

### 3.2. "Mental Health Days"
SuperTech offers **2 fully paid Mental Health Days** per year, separate from standard PTO and sick leave. These do not require a medical certificate but must be logged in *Workday* as "Mental Health Leave".

---

## 4. Overtime and Private Exits

### 4.1. Overtime Policy
SuperTech strictly discourages systemic overtime. However, during critical project phases, overtime may be necessary.
* **Approval:** All overtime **must be pre-approved** in writing (email or Jira ticket comment) by the Project Manager. Unapproved overtime will not be compensated.
* **Compensation:** Pre-approved overtime can be compensated in two ways:
  1. Time in lieu (1 hour of overtime = 1.5 hours of time off), to be taken within the same billing month.
  2. Financial compensation at 150% of the standard hourly rate.

### 4.2. Private Exits during Working Hours
We understand that personal matters (e.g., doctor's appointments, school runs) happen during the day.
* **Rules:** You can leave for up to 2 hours during the workday, provided you do not miss mandatory meetings.
* **Making up time:** The time must be made up within the same work week. 
* **Communication:** Always inform your team on Slack (using the 🏃‍♂️ emoji status) and block the time out in your Google Calendar so others know you are unavailable.
