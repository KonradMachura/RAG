# SuperTech Employee Handbook: IT and Security Policy
*Last updated: March 2025*

## 1. Hardware Policy

SuperTech equips employees with high-quality tools to ensure productivity.

### 1.1. Standard Kit
Upon hiring, employees choose between a MacBook Pro 14" (M-series) or a Dell XPS 15. All employees receive a 27" Dell Ultrasharp monitor, a wireless mouse, and a keyboard.

### 1.2. Remote Office Equipment
As mentioned in the Work Model policy, fully remote employees receive a $500 stipend for additional equipment (e.g., ergonomic chairs).

### 1.3. IT Support
All hardware and software issues must be reported by creating a ticket in *Jira Service Desk*. Critical issues (e.g., complete laptop failure) should be escalated by calling the IT Hotline (+1 800 555 0199).

---

## 2. Data Security and Privacy

Protecting client and company data is paramount.

### 2.1. Password Policy
* Minimum 14 characters.
* Must be rotated every 90 days.
* All employees must use the company-provided password manager (*1Password*).
* Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) is strictly enforced on all company accounts via the *Okta* Verify app.

### 2.2. Public Networks
When working remotely from cafes, airports, or hotels, employees **must** use the SuperTech Corporate VPN (Cisco Secure Client) at all times.

### 2.3. Personal Use of Devices
Company laptops are intended for business use. Minimal personal use (e.g., reading news, personal email) is tolerated, provided it does not interfere with work or violate the Acceptable Use Policy (e.g., no downloading of pirated software or visiting malicious sites).

### 2.4. Data Storage
All company documents must be stored on Google Workspace (Drive) or Confluence. Saving sensitive company files locally to the laptop's hard drive is prohibited.
